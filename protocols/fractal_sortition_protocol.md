# Fractal Sortition Protocol

## Purpose

To describe a governance method compatible with distributed seed networks and other bioregional stewardship systems.

## Core idea

Fractal sortition combines:
- random selection
- rotating participation
- structured deliberation
- escalation across scales

## Why use it

This approach may reduce:
- concentration of power
- popularity contests
- campaign incentives
- fixed gatekeeping roles

## Suggested council layers

1. Local node council
2. Sub-bioregional council
3. Regional guild council
4. Inter-bioregional coordination council

## Decision record requirements

A council decision should include:
- who was selected
- what scope they represented
- what decision was made
- rationale
- duration of validity
- review date

## Machine-readable output

Council outcomes should be published using:
- `council_decision.schema.json`

## Important note

Sortition does not remove the need for trust, ethics, or stewardship culture.

It only helps structure legitimacy more fairly.
