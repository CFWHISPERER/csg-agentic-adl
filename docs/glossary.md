# Glossary

## ADL
Agent Definition Language. A structured way to describe agent roles, inputs, outputs, actions, and constraints.

## Stewardship Event
A machine-readable record of a real-world ecological or governance action.

## Seed Accession
A uniquely tracked seed variety or seed lot with identifying and descriptive metadata.

## Bioregion
An ecological region defined by watershed, climate, flora, fauna, and landform rather than political borders alone.

## Fractal Sortition
A governance method combining random selection and structured deliberation across multiple scales.

## Polycentric
A system with many centers of decision-making rather than a single central authority.

## Federation
A way for multiple semi-independent nodes or groups to interoperate using shared standards.

## Provenance
The documented origin and history of a record, decision, or seed accession.
